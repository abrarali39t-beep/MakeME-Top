Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my business to MakeMeTop?
Tap the + button, fill in your business details, upload images, and submit for review. Our team approves listings within 24 hours. Once live, your profile appears on Google Search.
How do I edit my business profile?
Open your business from Dashboard → tap the Edit (pencil) icon in the top right. Update all details including images.
Will my business appear on Google?
Yes! Once approved, your business profile is indexed by Google with full Schema markup — your name, address, phone, and reviews all help you rank in Google Search.
Why is my listing pending?
All listings go through manual review for quality and authenticity. This typically takes 12–24 hours on business days.
Is MakeMeTop free?
Yes! Basic listing is completely free forever. Premium plans start at ₹499/month for priority placement and more visibility.
Can I edit or delete my review?
Yes! On any review you wrote, you'll see Edit and Delete buttons. Tap them to update or remove your review.
Can I submit only one review per business?
Yes. Each user can submit only one review per business. This keeps reviews fair and authentic.

Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2025 · MBP – MakeMeTop Business Profile · makemetop.in

1. Information We Collect

When you use MBP, we may collect: your name, email address, and profile photo when you sign up; business information you submit (name, address, phone, photos); reviews and ratings you post; and general usage data via Firebase Analytics.


2. How We Use It

Your data is used to create and display your MBP listing on Google Search, enable customer reviews, send you email notifications about your listing, and provide live chat support. We do not sell your data to third parties.


3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Firestore) with strict security rules. Business images are stored on Google Drive. Only you can access and edit your own listings. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit.


4. Your Rights

You can edit or delete your reviews at any time from the app. You can delete your business listing from your Dashboard. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact us via live chat in the app.


5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save draft listings and install preferences. Firebase uses session cookies to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising cookies or sell your browsing data.


6. Contact

For any privacy concerns or data deletion requests, contact us via live chat in the MBP app or email us at support@makemetop.in.
