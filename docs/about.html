What is MakeMe Top Business Profile (MBP)?

MakeMe Top Business Profile — known as MBP — is the #1 verified online directory for travel agencies, resorts, hotels, houseboats, tour operators, adventure companies, and hospitality businesses across Jammu & Kashmir, India.

Every listing is manually verified by our team, indexed on Google Search with full Schema.org LocalBusiness markup, and made discoverable to tourists planning trips to Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonamarg, Dal Lake, Yusmarg, Doodhpathri, Gurez, Lolab Valley, Bhaderwah and all of J&K.
