Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2025 · MBP – MakeMe Top Business Profile · makemetop.in

⚠️ Please Read Before Paying

The MBP listing fee is ₹999/year paid via UPI. Payments are processed manually. Please read this policy carefully to understand when refunds are available.

1. Listing Fee Overview

To publish a verified business listing on MakeMe Top Business Profile (MBP), a one-time annual fee of ₹999 is charged. This fee covers manual verification, approval, Google indexing, and all MBP features for 12 months from the date of listing approval.

2. When Refunds Are Available

✅ Full Refund – Payment Not Verified

If you submitted a valid payment screenshot and transaction ID but your listing was not approved within 7 business days due to our error or inability to verify the payment, you are entitled to a full refund of ₹999.

✅ Full Refund – Duplicate Payment

If you accidentally paid twice for the same listing (same account, same business name), the duplicate payment will be refunded in full within 5–7 business days.

✅ Full Refund – Technical Failure

If a confirmed technical error on our platform caused your payment to be deducted but the submission failed, you are entitled to a full refund upon verification.

3. When Refunds Are NOT Available

❌ No Refund – Listing Rejected for Policy Violation

If your listing is rejected because it violates our Terms & Conditions (e.g., fake business, incorrect information, prohibited content), no refund will be issued.

❌ No Refund – Change of Mind

Once a listing is approved and published, no refund will be issued for change of mind, business closure, or preference to use a different platform.

❌ No Refund – Payment Screenshot Not Valid

If you submit a fraudulent, edited, or unverifiable payment screenshot, your listing will be rejected and no refund will be issued.

❌ No Refund – After 30 Days

Refund requests submitted more than 30 days after the original payment date will not be accepted.

4. Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial refunds for unused portions of your listing period (e.g., if you decide to cancel mid-year).

5. How to Request a Refund

1
Contact Support

Email support@makemetop.in or use the in-app live chat within 30 days of payment.

2
Provide Details

Include your registered email, transaction ID/UTR number, the business name, and the reason for your refund request.

3
Verification

Our team will review your request within 3–5 business days and notify you of the outcome.

4
Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original UPI account used for payment.

6. Payment Security Note

All payments are processed via UPI — India's secure real-time payment system. MBP does not store your bank account details, card numbers, or UPI PIN. If you believe you made a payment in error or were defrauded, contact your bank or UPI provider directly in addition to contacting us.

7. Subscription Renewal

MBP listings are not auto-renewed. There are no automatic charges to your account. You will need to manually renew your listing before its expiry date. Expired listings may be unpublished after a grace period.

8. Contact

For all refund enquiries: support@makemetop.in
Or use the live chat in the MBP app.
