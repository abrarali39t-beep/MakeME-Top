Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 · MBP – MakeMe Top Business Profile · makemetop.in
This Privacy Policy describes how MakeMe Top Business Profile (MBP), operated at makemetop.in, collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our platform. By using MBP, you agree to this policy.

1. Who We Are

MakeMe Top Business Profile (referred to as "MBP", "we", "our", or "us") is an online directory platform for verified travel agencies, resorts, and tourism businesses in Kashmir, India. The platform is accessible at makemetop.in.

For privacy-related queries, contact us at support@makemetop.in.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you sign up using Google or email, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo (if provided by Google). This data is stored securely in Firebase Authentication and Firestore.

2.2 Business Listing Data

When you create a business profile (MBP), we collect: business name, type, description, address, phone number, WhatsApp number, website, and uploaded images. This information is publicly displayed once your listing is approved.

2.3 Payment Information

When you make a UPI payment, we collect: your transaction ID/UTR number, amount paid, payment screenshot (uploaded by you), and contact number. We do not store credit card numbers, bank account details, or other sensitive financial credentials. Payments are processed independently via your UPI app.

2.4 Reviews

Reviews you write are associated with your display name and user ID and are publicly visible on the relevant business listing.

2.5 Chat & Support Messages

Messages sent via the live chat or contact form are stored to provide support and are accessible only to you and our admin team.

2.6 Usage Data

We may collect basic analytics data (page views, interactions) using Firebase Analytics and/or Google Analytics to improve the platform. This data is anonymized and aggregated.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your MBP account
  • To publish and manage your business listing on our directory
  • To verify your payment and approve your listing within 48 hours
  • To allow other users to contact your business via the platform
  • To display customer reviews on your profile
  • To send you transactional emails (account creation, listing approval) — you may opt out at any time
  • To improve the platform's performance, features and user experience
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Firebase / Google: Our infrastructure provider (auth, database, hosting). Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Apps Script: Used to send transactional emails. No marketing use.
  • Legal obligation: We may disclose data if required by Indian law or a valid court order.

Business listing data (name, address, phone, type, images, services) is publicly visible on your approved MBP profile and may be indexed by search engines including Google.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Business listings are retained as long as you maintain an active subscription or until you request deletion. Payment records are retained for 7 years for legal compliance. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by us
  • Rectify inaccurate data in your profile
  • Delete your account and business listings
  • Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Data portability — request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact support@makemetop.in or use the in-app support chat.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

MBP uses browser local storage to save user preferences (such as the install prompt dismissal status, draft listing data, and offer timer). We do not use third-party tracking cookies. Google Analytics and Firebase may set cookies governed by Google's policies.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase Authentication, Firestore Security Rules, HTTPS encryption, and strict data access controls. However, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your account password confidential.

9. Children's Privacy

MBP is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us immediately.

10. International Users

MBP is operated from India. If you access MBP from the USA, UK, UAE, Canada, Australia, Israel, or any other country, your data is transferred to and processed in India in accordance with Indian data protection laws. By using MBP, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by a revised "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related queries: support@makemetop.in
